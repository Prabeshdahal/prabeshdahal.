CoHelp

By this hard time, we all are affected directly or indirectly. So, for who are affected and in need of immediate help, they do not have much options to choose from. And the same way, if someone has something to offer, they do not have much options either. To address this issue a bit, we started a small initiative where someone can ask for help and some other person can be help for them. This is just the platform to ask and provide possible help. Asking a help here doesn't mean we will get help. But obviously there will be more chance to be helped. By the use of this app, if even a single person got benefited, I will be happy the most.
